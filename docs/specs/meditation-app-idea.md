# meditation app idea

**date:** 2026-03-27
**status:** idea / early concept
**source:** T

---

## concept

an app that uses AI to generate personalized meditations based on what you want or need right now.

**examples:**
- "I'd like a meditation based on emotional inquiry that's 20 minutes long"
- "I'm very stressed and want a meditation based on this journal entry"
- custom duration, style, and focus — generated on the fly

## why it's valuable

- every meditation app today serves pre-recorded content (calm, headspace, insight timer)
- this would be genuinely personalized — not "pick from a menu" but "tell me what you need right now"
- journal integration is a killer feature — your own writing as the seed for your practice
- bridges the gap between therapy/journaling and meditation
- could use voice synthesis for guided audio or text-based for silent meditation prompts

## open questions

- audio generation vs. text-based guidance?
- standalone app or feature within lungful?
- what AI capabilities are needed? (LLM for content, TTS for voice, maybe fine-tuned on meditation scripts)
- privacy implications of journal entry access
- monetization: subscription makes more sense here than pay-once (ongoing AI costs)

## relationship to lungful

lungful is the metronome — simple, offline, pay-once. this would be the composer — AI-powered, connected, subscription. could be a separate product under the halfday brand or a premium tier within lungful.
